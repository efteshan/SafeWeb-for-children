ABOUT THIS RESEARCH

Why Children's Online Safety Matters

Children learn, socialize, and explore through the web. With that opportunity comes risk. Some messages, content, and contact that can harm wellbeing and trust.

This project focuses on how the WWW intersects with childhood development, the warning signs communities should recognize, and the practical actions that families, schools, and policymakers can take.

We combine clear risk analysis with protection strategies so children gain confidence online while adults gain measurable tools to support them.

  • Risk increases when children lack privacy boundaries
  • Education reduces fear by teaching safe choices
  • Parental tools work best with family rules
  • Laws support safer platforms for minors
