Statistics & Research Data

Between 20% and 40% of adolescents report experiencing cyberbullying, while 1 in 3 children encounter online sexual content.

Age Group Cyberbullying (%) Inappropriate Content (%) Predator Contact (%) Screen Addiction (%)
6 - 8 years 12% 28% 4% 35%
9 - 11 years 27% 45% 9% 52%
12 - 14 years 48% 67% 18% 71%
15 - 17 years 59% 72% 23% 68%
18+ years 38% 55% 14% 60%

The Internet Watch Foundation processed 255,588 URLs containing abuse material in 2022, a 35% rise year on year.

Internet Gaming Disorder prevalence ranges from 1.6% to 8.5% depending on the population.
