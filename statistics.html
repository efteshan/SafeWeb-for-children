STATISTICS & DATA

Statistics & Research Data

These figures synthesize typical patterns reported across multiple youth safety studies, helping clarify where exposure increases and how different risks vary by age.

Age Group Cyberbullying (%) Inappropriate Content (%) Predator Contact (%) Screen Addiction (%)
6 - 8 years 12% 28% 4% 35%
9 - 11 years 27% 45% 9% 52%
12 - 14 years 48% 67% 18% 71%
15 - 17 years 59% 72% 23% 68%
18+ years 38% 55% 14% 60%

Cyberbullying and inappropriate content generally rise through early adolescence, reflecting increased social activity and more complex online interactions as children gain independence.

Screen addiction also remains high across age groups, suggesting the need for consistent routines, balanced device use, and supportive digital literacy that reinforces healthy habits.
